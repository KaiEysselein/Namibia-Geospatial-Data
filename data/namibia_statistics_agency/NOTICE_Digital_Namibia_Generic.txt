PURPOSE
-------
This notice accompanies spatial data downloaded from the Namibia Statistics Agency
(NSA) Digital Namibia portal. It provides context on attribution, reuse, and licensing
status as understood from publicly available NSA and NSDI policy documents.

SOURCE
------
Publisher: Namibia Statistics Agency (NSA)
Portal: Digital Namibia (https://digitalnamibia.nsa.org.na/)
Agency Website: https://nsa.org.na/
Contact: nsdi@nsa.org.na ; info@nsa.org.na

RECOMMENDED ATTRIBUTION
-----------------------
If you use, display, or analyse this data, include the following attribution:
  “Source: Namibia Statistics Agency (NSA) — Digital Namibia portal (accessed 2025-11-07).”

REUSE & LICENSING STATUS
------------------------
• As of 2025-11-07, the Digital Namibia portal and the NSA website do not publish an explicit
  open-data licence (e.g., Creative Commons or Open Government Licence).
• The NSA’s NSDI Policy specifies that spatial data custodians must protect State
  copyright and define reuse terms through licensing agreements.
• Therefore, redistribution, republishing, or commercial reuse of this data may require
  written permission from NSA or the relevant custodian.
• This dataset is shared for informational, academic, or interoperability purposes only.
  No claim of open or unrestricted licence is implied.
